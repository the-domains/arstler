A-Zdvent calendar: Miss Blanche Chair by Shiro Kuramata

Transparent resin blocks peppered with flowers form the seat and arms of the Miss Blanche Chair by Shiro Kuramata - letter M in our alphabetical Christmas countdown. Japanese designer Shiro Kuramata created the Miss Blanche Chair in 1988, three years before his death.
