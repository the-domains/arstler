Clo'e Floirat's charming drawings comment on contemporary cultural events, often with a satirical edge. Christian Wassmann, '"Dodecahedron" chandelier and optical instrument', 2014, Watermill Center Summer Benefit Auction 2016 | Artsy

Architect and designer Christian Wassmann began his career at a young age in Switzerland. Interested in the intersection between the arts and architecture, his currently New York-based practice (housed in his Studio Christian Wassmann) focuses on the interconnections between art forms. The firm's ideology is guided by the design principle "form performs function," rather than the modernist mantra "form follows function."
