Why I Consider Burning Man the Greatest Cultural Movement of Our Time

Burning Man has created something like a parallel art world. Over the years, I have watched many artists find their place at the event and build careers through their relationship to it. Kate Raudenbush's Helios (2016) drew inspiration from the theme of Leonardo da Vinci's Workshop, and sought to fuse art and technology.
