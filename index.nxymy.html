American Flag (from America: Now and Here portfolio) by Vik Muniz | Paddle8

Vik Muniz has held a lifelong fascination with iconic denotation, ephemerality, and photographic representation. Here, he presents a c-print of a United States flag composed of many multicolored plants. This is his contribution to the "America: Now and Here" portfolio, a set of thirteen prints by prominent artists made to accompany Eric Fischl's 2011 traveling exhibition meant to stir conversations about national identity and the arts.
