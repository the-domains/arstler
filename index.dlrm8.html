Donald Baechler, 'Cat', 2016, Watermill Center Summer Benefit Auction 2016 | Artsy

Donald Baechler's oeuvre exists between the obvious and the obscure, in which symbols of childhood nostalgia are brought to life in strong line and silhouette. Considered to be an art world prodigy, Baechler was an active member of the East Village art scene in 1980s alongside Jean Michel Basquiat and Keith Haring.
